Story of Jiapo

Jiapo, a cherished restaurant located in Taoyaun, has carved out a special place in the hearts of locals and visitors alike with its delectable Hakka Pork on Rice, mouth watering mochi and aromatic coffee. This family-owned establishment is not just about serving food; but a celebration of Hakka culture and heritage through family recipes passed down through generations
